Artisan Mechanical Keyboards

A little backstory...

I started my keyboard journey like a lot of people, playing PC games. I used to be a console only player as a kid but as I got older and needed to do school work on a computer, it was only natural that I start picking up on PC gaming. My first board was a Razer mechanical keyboard with the green clicky switches and from there it's only been down hill. At the time I was not aware of an enthusiast community, only mass production prebuilt "gamer" keyboards. At the time I was happy and enjoyed trying out new boards every few years until I stubbled upon "custom" keyboards.

Today and the future!

With my years of experience and experimenting with many different products, I can confidently say that prebuilt keyboards from companies like Logitech, Razer, and Corsair aren't worth the price. I unfortunately had to learn this the hard way, but with the keyboard enthusiast community growing and the demand for increasingly higher quality products growing, I believe that it's easier than ever to get into custom keyboards. Though I say it is "easier" now than ever before, there are so many choices, variables, and preferences that it can easily be overwhelming, I find myself lost in all the options from time to time. With that in mind, my goal is to offer my experiences to those who are looking for a streamlined experiences. If you would like me to build you a keyboard, please refer to the contact section.
